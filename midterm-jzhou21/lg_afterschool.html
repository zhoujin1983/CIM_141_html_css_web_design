After-School Services(K-6th)

Arts
Age appropriate art projects with introduction to sketching, painting, watercolor and more wonderful programs designed for students to explore the wonderful things of the art world. Check out these 45 minutes classes.
Music
Each student is unique, our experienced teacher will help the students become confident and passionate about music. Students can learn Violin, Cello, Flute, Keyboard and Guitar. Check out these 45 minutes classes.
Sports
We offer a variety of sports classes to teach children the core skills they need to get in the game, such as balance, strength, cooperation, and coordination. Students can learn soccer, tennis and basketball. Check out these 45 minutes classes.
